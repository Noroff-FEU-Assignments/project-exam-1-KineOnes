Hello there! I'm Lucinda Ones, a passionate ceramic artist and instructor dedicated to bringing the joy of working with clay to life. Nestled in the heart of Ski, my studio is a haven for creativity and self-expression. With hands immersed in the soothing embrace of clay, I craft intricate and unique ceramic pieces that tell stories of both tradition and innovation. From functional tableware to sculptural wonders, each creation is infused with my love for the craft and a touch of whimsy.
